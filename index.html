Fighting AI Slop

We hate AI slop. We're drowning in it. Soulless generated images, copy-paste "art," algorithmic garbage flooding every feed and timeline. It's cheap, it's easy, and it's killing creativity.

Made By Humans is our answer. We celebrate art that's intentionally simple, authentic, and real. Art that doesn't need AI. Art that doesn't need fancy prompts or rendering farms. Art that just needs a human with a marker, chalk, or crayon.

This is a protest. Every piece of human-made art is a middle finger to AI slop. Every artist paid is proof that humans still create value. Every imperfect line is evidence that soul matters more than polish.

We're not fighting AI by complaining. We're fighting AI by DOING something. By putting money in the hands of real artists. By creating a global movement of human-made art. By proving that people care about authenticity.

Join the resistance. Create art. Pay artists. Reject AI slop.

An Individual

A protest against ai slop that everyone can take part in

What is an individual?

AI is taking over the world. Every day, machines get smarter, faster, more capable. They can write, create, and even think. But what about us? What about the messy, beautiful, imperfect human touch?

We asked ourselves: What if we created a universal movement to celebrate humanity? Something so simple, so pure, that it could only come from human hands. Something that says "we were here, we created this, we are real."

That's when Made By Humans was born. Not by algorithm. Not by neural network. But by human hands holding pencils, brushes, and chalk across the globe.

Made By Humans is a sign of love. It's humanity distilled into its simplest form—real people creating real art. Made By Humans transcends language, culture, and borders. It's universal, yet deeply personal with every stroke.

We paid real people in different countries to create art in their area. Not because it's perfect, but because it's human. Each piece is unique. Each carries the soul of the artist who made it. Each is proof that in an age of artificial intelligence, the human spirit still creates, still connects, still matters.

Every crooked line, every wobbly circle, every smudge of chalk or streak of paint—these aren't mistakes. They're proof of life. They're proof that humans, with all our imperfections, are still here, still creating, still leaving our mark on this world.

Made By Humans isn't fighting AI. It's reminding us what makes us human in the first place. The ability to create something with our hands, to connect with strangers across the world, to turn a simple idea into a global movement of love and creativity.

So when you see our work, remember: this wasn't generated. This wasn't optimized. This wasn't perfect. This was made by a human, for humans, with love.

Made By Humans is our reminder that humanity is still worth celebrating. 💙
